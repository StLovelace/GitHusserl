    Authors and Contributors

    In 2010, the monks and nuns of St. Lovelace (@stlovelacemonastery) began working toward the Open Husserl Archives. In 2013 they founded GitHusserl to begin to disseminate the seeds of this work.

    Contribute and Use.

    We've crafted some places to begin and look forward to your help to begin again. The texts in the Open Husserl Archives are GNU GPL.
